Is there some way I can experience the game in the meantime?

You're in luck! This patch actually is a continuation of a YouTube series I did from 2020-2024 where I did a 100% playthrough of the game and translated everything along the way. While the translation in these videos is a first draft that can be a bit rough in places, it is accurate for the most part and got better as it went on. You can find it here.

Currently, the patch for Episodes 1-2 is released, so if you want to continue from where the patch left off, you should start with part 64.

How many Episodes are in the game?

The game has seven Episodes in total.

Does the patch work on real hardware?

Yes, so long as your PSP or PS Vita is jailbroken to play homebrew and mods, the Lucky Star: Ryouou Gakuen Outousai English patch will run just fine on real hardware.

Does the patch work on the PS2 version?

No, the patch only works on the PSP version of Lucky Star: Ryouou Gakuen Outousai, known as Lucky Star: Ryouou Gakuen Outousai Portable.

Will you make a patch for the PS2 version?

There are currently no plans to make a patch for the PS2 version.

The reason for this is primarily because of the additional effort required to maintain two versions, as well as the fact that a lot of work done on the PSP version would not work on the PS2 version due to the two versions not being exactly the same. The PSP version is the definitive version of the game with additional content and quality of life changes, so my focus is on providing the highest quality patch I can for that version rather than spread myself thin.
